## 9. Nutrition & body composition

- **Protein** 1.8–2.2 g/kg/day for recovery and 82→76 kg recomposition.
- **Deficit only in W1–8**, gentle (−0.3 to −0.5 kg/wk); fuel quality/long days fully. Hold weight steady from W9 — do not diet into races.
- **In-run fueling**: practice every Saturday from W5; build 50 → 90 g CHO/h. Rehearse exact race gels/drink at W7, W9, W13.
- **Easy Z1 runs** may be fasted to support fat oxidation (never quality days).
- **Hydration/electrolytes** emphasized for August heat and long days.
- **Carb-load** 36–48 h before Palencia (W11) and TP60 (W15).

## 10. Monitoring & adjustment

- Log **morning resting HR** daily; HRV if available.
- Expect same-pace HR to drift down across Phase 1–2 (aerobic improvement).
- Long runs completed without bonk = fueling dialed.
- **Red flags** → insert recovery / cut Sunday B2B: sustained RHR +7, broken sleep, persistent soreness, rising same-pace HR.
- Use the dashboard's plan-vs-actual chart weekly; adjust the following week's load to reality, not ego.
